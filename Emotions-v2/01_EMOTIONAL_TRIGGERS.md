# 01 — Emotional Triggers

The emotion palette. This is the canonical trigger library the rest of the brain points to (`Ideas-v2/02`, `ViralHooks-v2`, `WritingSystem-v2/04`). A promo lands because it makes the viewer *feel* something — pick 1–2 triggers per promo.

**Role in the app:** the emotional layer of the idea formula and the script. Honest use only.

---

## The Triggers

### Frustration → Relief
- **What:** name the pain sharply, then lift it.
- **Neutral example:** "Sick of chasing late invoices? They can chase themselves."
- **Best beat:** Hook + Turn.

### Transformation
- **What:** a clear before → after.
- **Example:** "From spreadsheet chaos to paid in a day."
- **Best beat:** Turn + Proof.

### Social Proof
- **What:** others like them already got the result.
- **Example:** "Freelancers are getting paid the same day they invoice."
- **Best beat:** Proof.

### Authority / Credibility
- **What:** proven, tested, trustworthy.
- **Example:** "Built by people who got tired of chasing their own payments."
- **Best beat:** Proof.

### Curiosity
- **What:** open a loop worth resolving.
- **Example:** "There's one reason you get paid late — and it's not the client."
- **Best beat:** Hook.

### Aspiration
- **What:** the better version of their life/work.
- **Example:** "Spend your time creating, not collecting."
- **Best beat:** Turn + CTA.

### Loss / Cost of Inaction (honest)
- **What:** the real, true cost of not acting — never fabricated urgency.
- **Example:** "Every late invoice is billable hours you'll never get back."
- **Best beat:** Problem.

### Belonging / Identity
- **What:** "this is for people like me."
- **Example:** "For freelancers who'd rather build than bill."
- **Best beat:** Hook + CTA.

---

## Priority for Promos

When choosing, lead with these — they convert hardest in 30 seconds:

| Rank | Trigger |
|---|---|
| 1 | Frustration → Relief |
| 2 | Transformation |
| 3 | Social Proof |
| 4 | Authority |

Curiosity and Identity are strong openers; Loss and Aspiration are accents — use them, but don't build the whole promo on them.

---

## Rules

- **1–2 triggers per promo.** More dilutes the feeling.
- **Balance.** Don't stack fear/loss — pair any hard trigger with hope or relief.
- **Honest only.** Real proof, real stakes. No fabricated FOMO, fake scarcity, or invented numbers.
