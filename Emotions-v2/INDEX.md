## Quick Rules
- **1–2 triggers per promo** — never a stack.
- **Pair hard with soft** — Loss/Frustration must resolve into Relief/Aspiration.
- **Default trio:** Frustration→Relief (hook) · Transformation (turn) · Social Proof (proof).
- **Honest only** — no fabricated fear, scarcity, or numbers.
