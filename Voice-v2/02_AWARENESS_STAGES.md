# 02 — Awareness & Delivery

Where the viewer is on the awareness ladder changes how the voice opens and how hard the CTA pushes. The full ladder lives in the ICP file; this covers how it affects *delivery*.

**Role in the app:** adjusts the opening and CTA intensity of the promo.
**Canonical ICP awareness stages:** `ICPOffer-v2/01_ICP_PROFILE.md` (don't duplicate — reference it).

---

## How awareness shifts the voice

| Stage | How the promo opens | CTA push |
|---|---|---|
| **Unaware** | name a hidden cost they haven't noticed | soft — spark curiosity |
| **Problem Aware** | say their pain out loud, in their words | soft-medium — offer relief |
| **Solution Aware** | contrast with the way they're doing it now | medium — differentiate + try |
| **Product Aware** | lead with proof + remove risk | direct |
| **Most Aware** | go straight to the offer | most direct |

---

## Rule

Awareness sets **intensity**, not tone. A Calm-Authority promo can be soft (unaware) or direct (most aware) — same voice, different push. CTA intensity detail lives in `CTA-v2/05_FUNNEL_STAGE_CTAS.md`.

Most promos the app makes target **Problem Aware / Solution Aware**, so the opening usually names the pain, and the CTA is medium-direct.
