# 01 — Voice Modes (Tone Dials)

The promo's voice isn't fixed — the app picks the tone that best fits what the founder is building, then layers it on the general foundation (see `10`). This file defines the tones to pick from.

**Role in the app:** sets the *tone* of the Stage 3 script and the voiceover delivery. Chosen per product (see `03`), not per a fixed persona.

---

## The General Foundation (always on)

Whatever tone is chosen, every promo is:

- **Clear** — one idea per line, plain words, nothing to decode.
- **Confident** — states things plainly, no hedging on what the product does.
- **Specific** — real numbers and details, never vague.
- **Honest** — no hype it can't back up, no fabricated proof or urgency.
- **Spoken** — written to be *heard*, in short lines that breathe.

The tone modes below adjust *flavor* on top of this foundation — they never override it.

---

## The 4 Tone Modes

### Calm Authority (default)
- **Feel:** the expert who's done it, unhurried, sure.
- **Fits:** productivity tools, B2B, fintech, developer tools, anything where trust matters most.
- **Sounds like:** "You're losing three hours a month to this. Here's how that stops."

### Warm & Friendly
- **Feel:** a helpful peer talking to you directly.
- **Fits:** consumer apps, wellness, education, creator tools, community products.
- **Sounds like:** "Getting paid shouldn't feel awkward. Let's make it simple."

### Bold & Energetic
- **Feel:** punchy, high-energy, momentum.
- **Fits:** launches, growth tools, bold consumer brands, anything young/fast.
- **Sounds like:** "Stop chasing invoices. Get paid in seconds. Let's go."

### Straightforward / No-Nonsense
- **Feel:** dry, plain, zero fluff — lets the facts hit.
- **Fits:** technical audiences, indie-hacker tools, anything where hype would backfire.
- **Sounds like:** "Send an invoice. Get paid faster. That's it."

---

## Rule

Pick **one** mode per promo (see `03` for how the app chooses). Never mix modes inside a single script — it muddies the delivery. When unsure, default to **Calm Authority**.
