# 08 — Voice Across the 30-Second Beats

How the voice actually shows up beat by beat. (This replaces the old tweet "viral patterns" — hook patterns now live in `ViralHooks-v2`; the script skeleton lives in `ICPOffer-v2/06_CONTENT_ANGLE_MAP`. This file is the voice layer over those beats.)

**Role in the app:** guides delivery across the five beats of every promo.

---

## The Beats, in Voice

| Time | Beat | What the voice does |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | **Hook** | grab — spoken pattern interrupt, straight at the viewer, one specific detail |
| 3–8s | **Problem** | twist the knife — one vivid, felt symptom in their words |
| 8–15s | **Turn** | shift the energy — "here's what's different / here's life instead" |
| 15–22s | **Proof** | steady and concrete — say the real number plainly, let it sit |
| 22–30s | **CTA** | direct and warm — one clear ask, land it, stop |

---

## Tone shifts within a promo (subtle)

Even in one tone mode, the energy moves:

- **Hook + Problem:** lean into the tension — this is where the viewer feels seen.
- **Turn:** lift — relief, momentum, possibility.
- **Proof:** settle — calm confidence; over-selling here reads as desperate.
- **CTA:** clear and inviting — not pushy, not passive.

---

## Rule

The tone mode (`01`/`03`) sets the overall flavor; this beat map sets how the energy rises and falls *within* the 30 seconds. One idea per beat, one ask at the end.
