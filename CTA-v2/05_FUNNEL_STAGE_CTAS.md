# 05 — CTA by Awareness Stage

The CTA's intensity should match how ready the viewer is. Same promo structure, different closing push.

**Role in the app:** the app sets CTA intensity from the ICP's awareness stage (`ICPOffer-v2/01_ICP_PROFILE`).

---

## The Match

| Awareness stage | Viewer mindset | CTA intensity | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Unaware** | doesn't know they have the problem | softest — invite curiosity | "See what you might be losing to late invoices." |
| **Problem Aware** | feels the pain | soft-medium — offer relief | "See how fast you could get paid." |
| **Solution Aware** | comparing options | medium — differentiate + try | "Try the one that chases payments for you." |
| **Product Aware** | knows you, hesitating | direct — remove risk | "Free until your first invoice gets paid. Start now." |
| **Most Aware** | ready | most direct | "Send your first invoice in two minutes — start free." |

---

## Rule

There is always exactly one ask — only the *push* changes. Colder viewer = lower-commitment first step; warmer viewer = straight to the action.

Most promos the app makes will target **Problem Aware / Solution Aware**, so the medium-intensity CTAs are the workhorses.
