# 08 — Worked CTA Examples

Neutral, ready-to-adapt CTA lines across offer types and awareness stages. Used as few-shot examples in the Stage 3 prompt. Product: **InvoiceZen.**

---

## Self-serve app

- **Most aware:** "Send your first invoice in two minutes — start free."
- **Solution aware:** "Try the invoicing app that chases late payments for you."
- **Problem aware:** "See how fast you could actually get paid."

---

## Paid product (trial)

- "Free for 14 days, cancel anytime — get your invoices sorted this week."

---

## Service / done-for-you

- "Book a 15-minute call and we'll set your invoicing up for you."

---

## Waitlist

- "Getting paid on time is almost here — join the early-access list."

---

## Free resource / community

- "Grab the free 'get paid faster' invoice template."

---

## Anti-examples (never generate)

- ❌ "Check out our features and learn more!" (vague, feature-led, no outcome)
- ❌ "Start free! Book a call! Follow us!" (stacked asks)
- ❌ "Only 3 spots left today!" (fabricated urgency)
- ❌ "You might want to give it a try." (hedged)
