## Quick Rules
- **One line = one breath** (5–12 words); label every beat with its time window.
- **~55–75 words total** for a natural 30-second read.
- **Numbers as spoken words** in the script ("eleven days"); numerals are fine only in on-screen captions.
- **No ALL-CAPS / arrows / bullets in the spoken lines** — those live in captions (`03`), not the voiceover.
- Delivery/tone comes from `Voice-v2`; structure of the beats from `ICPOffer-v2/06`.
