# Ideas-v2 — Index (App Angle Engine)

The idea engine that turns the marketing brain into **promo video angles** — the concepts that become hooks and scripts. Feeds Stage 3.

**Version:** 2.0 (app) · **Updated:** Jul 2026
**App users:** solo founders · vibe coders · indie hackers
**Output:** promo-video angles → hooks → 30-sec scripts + voiceover (NOT tweets)
**Examples:** neutral — *InvoiceZen, a simple invoicing app for freelancers.*

---

## ⚠️ Consolidated

The original was a 10-file tweet-idea system built on 3,777-tweet engagement data. Most of it (engagement tiers, X algo signals, content-mix %, real tweet examples, posting rules) was Twitter-only and has been **cut**. What remains is the reusable idea logic, retargeted to promo angles. Originals are in `_twitter_archive/`.

---

## The Core Formula

> **ICP Pain (or Desire) × Emotional Trigger × Reframe → Promo Angle**

---

## Pipeline

```
BRAIN (ICPOffer) → IDEAS (this folder): 5 angles → pick best
     → HOOK (ViralHooks) → SCRIPT (ICPOffer/06 beats) → VOICE (Voice-v2) → ElevenLabs
```

## Quick Rules
- **5 angles per run**, varied — different pains, triggers, reframes.
- **1–2 emotional triggers per angle**, **1 reframe + 1 lens**.
- **Honest only** — real pains, real claims, no fabricated stats or urgency.
- Every angle must pass all 4 validation checks before it becomes a script.
