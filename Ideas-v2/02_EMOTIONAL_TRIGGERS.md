# 02 — Emotional Triggers

The feelings that make a promo angle land. Pick one or two per angle — the trigger is what makes the viewer care.

**Role in the app:** the middle term of the idea formula (`01`).
**Note:** the fuller trigger library will live in `Emotions-v2` (canonical). This is the working set for idea generation.

---

## The Working Set

### Social Proof
- **What:** others like them already got the result.
- **Example:** "Freelancers are getting paid the same day they invoice."

### Authority / Credibility
- **What:** this is proven, backed, trustworthy.
- **Example:** "Built by people who got tired of chasing their own invoices."

### Transformation
- **What:** a clear before → after.
- **Example:** "From spreadsheet chaos to paid in a day."

### Frustration → Relief
- **What:** name the pain sharply, then lift it.
- **Example:** "No more awkward 'just following up' emails."

### Curiosity
- **What:** open a loop worth resolving.
- **Example:** "There's one reason freelancers get paid late — and it's not the client."

### Fear of Loss (honest only)
- **What:** the real cost of not acting.
- **Example:** "Every late invoice is billable hours you'll never get back."

### Belonging / Identity
- **What:** "this is for people like me."
- **Example:** "For freelancers who'd rather create than chase money."

---

## Rules

- **1–2 triggers per angle** — more than that dilutes it.
- **Honest only.** Real social proof, real stakes. Never fabricate numbers, reviews, or urgency.
- For most promos, lead with **Frustration→Relief, Transformation, or Social Proof** — they convert hardest in 30 seconds.
