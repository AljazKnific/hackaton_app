# 05 — Angle Validation

Run every angle through these before it becomes a hook/script. Skip a check and you risk a weak promo. (The old engagement/algorithm checks were Twitter-only and are cut.)

**Role in the app:** the gate between idea generation (`01`) and hook/script.

---

## Step 1 — ICP Relevance
**Ask:** does this hit a pain or desire the ICP genuinely feels?
- ✅ The viewer would think "that's exactly my problem."
- 🚩 Too broad (true for everyone = lands for no one), or a pain they don't actually have.

## Step 2 — Differentiation
**Ask:** is this fresh, or could it be any product's ad?
- ✅ A specific reframe, real detail, or angle they haven't heard.
- 🚩 Generic ("save time, work smarter") — regenerate with a sharper reframe (`03`).

## Step 3 — Video Fit
**Ask:** can this be told in 30 spoken seconds with a hook and one CTA?
- ✅ One clear idea, one pain, one payoff.
- 🚩 Needs setup, multiple ideas, or can't resolve in 30s → split into separate angles.

## Step 4 — Honesty
**Ask:** is every claim real?
- ✅ Real pain, real mechanism, real numbers (or none).
- 🚩 Invented stats, fake urgency, promises the product can't keep → cut or soften.

---

## Quality Bar

| Level | Standard |
|---|---|
| Minimum | specific enough to hook and script immediately |
| Optimal | makes the founder think "that's exactly how I'd pitch it" |

**Fails any step → regenerate the angle.**
