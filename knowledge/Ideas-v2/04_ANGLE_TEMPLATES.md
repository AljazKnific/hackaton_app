# 04 — Angle Templates

Fill-in starting points for promo angles. Recast from the old tweet templates into **video-angle** shapes. Use them to generate variety fast.

**Role in the app:** quick scaffolds for the idea formula (`01`). Each maps to a hook type in `ViralHooks-v2`.

---

## 1 — Built / Made
`We built [thing] that [specific result].`
- **Example:** "We built the invoicing app that gets freelancers paid eleven days faster."
- **Hook fit:** Simple-Change Transformation / Scale flex.

## 2 — Everyone Gets This Wrong
`Everyone thinks [belief]. Here's the truth.`
- **Example:** "Everyone thinks late payments are a client problem. They're a timing problem."
- **Hook fit:** Myth-Buster / Hidden Variable.

## 3 — Transformation
`From [before] to [after] in [timeframe].`
- **Example:** "From chasing invoices for weeks to paid in a day."
- **Hook fit:** Humble-Start Transformation / Before-After.

## 4 — The Real Reason
`There's one reason [problem happens] — and it's not what you think.`
- **Example:** "There's one reason freelancers get paid late — and it's not the client."
- **Hook fit:** Hidden Variable / Curiosity.

## 5 — The System
`Here's how [outcome] actually happens.`
- **Example:** "Here's how you get paid without ever sending a follow-up."
- **Hook fit:** How-It-Works / Redefinition.

## 6 — Frustration → Fix
`Sick of [pain]? Here's why it happens, and the fix.`
- **Example:** "Sick of awkward payment reminders? Here's the fix that sends them for you."
- **Hook fit:** Mirror / Direct Stakes.

## 7 — For People Like You
`For [specific identity] who [want outcome].`
- **Example:** "For freelancers who'd rather create than chase money."
- **Hook fit:** Mirror / Belonging.

## 8 — If I Started Over
`If I were [starting situation], here's exactly what I'd do.`
- **Example:** "If I were freelancing again, I'd never invoice from a spreadsheet."
- **Hook fit:** Underdog Proof / How-It-Works.

---

## Rule

A template is a starting point, not the finished angle. Always fill it with the founder's **real** pain, product, and (where available) real numbers.
