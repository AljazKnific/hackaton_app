# 03 — Reframe Techniques

The twist that makes an angle feel fresh instead of like every other ad. Every angle uses at least one.

**Role in the app:** the third term of the idea formula (`01`).

---

## The 5 Reframes

### Inversion
- **Move:** flip the common assumption.
- **Example:** "Getting paid faster isn't about better clients — it's about better timing."

### Simplification
- **Move:** complex problem → simple truth.
- **Example:** "Late payments come down to one thing: when the invoice lands."

### Elevation
- **Move:** tactic → bigger meaning.
- **Example:** "Your invoice isn't paperwork — it's the last impression you leave."

### Specificity
- **Move:** vague → concrete and real.
- **Example:** "Not 'get paid faster' — paid eleven days faster."

### Temporal
- **Move:** before vs. after / then vs. now.
- **Example:** "You used to chase payments for weeks. Now they chase themselves."

---

## The 3 Idea Lenses

Rotate these to generate variety. Each angle comes from **one** lens — mixing muddies it.

| Lens | Generates | Flavor |
|---|---|---|
| **Belief-Shift** | reframes, "you're thinking about this wrong" | changes how they see the problem |
| **How-It-Works** | mechanism, proof, systems | shows why it actually works |
| **Outcome** | the after-state, simplicity, the win | makes the result feel real |

---

## Applying it

> Pick pain/desire → pick trigger (`02`) → pick the reframe + lens that makes it fresh → write the one-line angle.
