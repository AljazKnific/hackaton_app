# 03 — Trigger Selection

How the app picks which emotional trigger(s) to use for a given promo — by the viewer's awareness stage and by where it lands in the script.

**Role in the app:** chooses triggers from `01` when generating an angle/script.

---

## By Awareness Stage

| Stage | Lead trigger | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Unaware** | Curiosity / Loss (honest) | they don't feel the problem yet — surface the hidden cost |
| **Problem Aware** | Frustration → Relief | name the pain they already feel |
| **Solution Aware** | Transformation / Authority | show your way is different and works |
| **Product Aware** | Social Proof / Authority | prove it, remove doubt |
| **Most Aware** | Aspiration / Identity | connect to the win, then ask |

(Awareness stages: `ICPOffer-v2/01_ICP_PROFILE`.)

---

## By Beat

| Beat | Trigger that fits |
|---|---|
| Hook (0–3s) | Curiosity · Frustration · Identity |
| Problem (3–8s) | Loss / cost of inaction |
| Turn (8–15s) | Transformation · Aspiration |
| Proof (15–22s) | Social Proof · Authority |
| CTA (22–30s) | Aspiration · Identity |

---

## Selection Rules

- **Pick 1–2 per promo**, not more.
- **Pair hard with soft** — if the hook uses Loss or Frustration, the turn must offer Relief or Aspiration.
- **Match the stage** — a Most-Aware viewer doesn't need the problem re-explained; an Unaware one isn't ready for the offer.
- **Honest only** (see `01`, `02`).

---

## Quick Default

For the typical promo (Problem/Solution-Aware viewer): **Frustration→Relief in the hook, Transformation in the turn, Social Proof in the proof beat.** That trio converts reliably.
