# Voice-v2 — Index (App Promo Voice)

How every promo should *sound* — the writing tone and spoken cadence the app applies before the ElevenLabs voiceover. This is the app's signature: voice is the differentiator.

**Version:** 2.0 (app) · **Updated:** Jul 2026
**App users:** solo founders · vibe coders · indie hackers
**Output:** spoken 30-sec promo scripts + voiceover (NOT tweets)
**Examples:** neutral — *InvoiceZen, a simple invoicing app for freelancers.*

---

## ⚠️ This is no longer a personal Twitter voice

The original was Rofi's personal tweet persona (lowercase, insider/contrarian mix, edge, Twitter formats). This version is a **generalized promo voiceover guide**: a general foundation + a tone picked to fit each founder's product. Rofi's personal voice is preserved in `_twitter_archive/` and in the separate `tweet-voice` skill — **do not recompile `tweet-voice` from these app files.**

---

## How the voice works

> **Best-fit promo voice = General Foundation + Tone Mode (chosen by product) + Spoken Cadence**

The app reads what the founder is building, picks the tone that fits, and combines it with the always-on foundation (clear, confident, specific, honest, spoken).

## Quick Rules
- **No fixed persona** — tone is picked per product (`03`), on one honest foundation (`01`).
- **Spoken first** — short lines, one idea per breath; cut lowercase/punctuation tricks.
- **Sentence length inverts** — 5–12 words per line, not the old 25–30.
- **Numbers as spoken words** ("eleven days," not "11d").
- **Never fabricate** numbers, proof, or urgency.
- **One tone mode per script**, one CTA at the end.
