# 03 — Strategy Principles

The marketing thinking the app bakes into every promo. These are the "why it works" rules behind the pipeline — the standard every generated promo is held to.

**Role in the app:** the guiding principles for Stage 2/3 generation.

---

## The Principles

1. **ICP-first.** Speak to one specific person, not "everyone." A promo for everyone lands for no one. (`ICPOffer-v2/01`)

2. **One idea per promo.** A 30-second video makes a single point. Extra ideas become separate promos.

3. **Outcome over feature.** Sell what the viewer *gets* ("get paid faster"), never the mechanism ("automated reminders"). Features go in the middle, outcomes in the hook and CTA.

4. **Meet them at their awareness stage.** An unaware viewer needs the hidden cost; a ready one needs the offer. Match the opening and the push to where they are. (`Emotions-v2/03`)

5. **Show the before → after.** The signature move: contrast the vague old offer with the sharpened one. It's the proof the brain did something. (`ICPOffer-v2/05`)

6. **Voice is the differentiator.** The promo is delivered as a voiceover — tone is picked to fit the product, and every line is written to be *heard*. (`Voice-v2`)

7. **Honest persuasion only.** Real pains, real numbers, real proof, real urgency — or none. Fabrication is the one thing that breaks the whole system.

8. **Always one CTA.** Earn the attention, then point at a single low-friction next step. (`CTA-v2`)

---

## The Standard

Every promo should make the viewer feel: *"That's my problem — and that's the fix."* If it doesn't make them feel seen and then move them toward one clear action, it isn't done.
